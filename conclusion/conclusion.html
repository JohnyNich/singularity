Conclusion

In conclusion, the question of if technology has gone too far comes down to your perception of technology, your ethical views, and just your overall opinoin on the subject

I've created a list of pros and cons of technology here

Good:

  • Technology can help us mass manufacture products and end sweat shops and child slavery
  • 3D printing could help 3D print body parts
  • With the help of AI, we could have robots do the dangerous jobs and not put human lives at risk.
  • AI could help speed up jobs that we don't wan

Bad:

  • Encryption means that terroist groups can send information and messages about plans without anyone knowing.

  • AIs could become smarter than humans (This is known as the singularity, and this is incredibly hypothetical).
